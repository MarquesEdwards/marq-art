My name is Marques Edwards and I am a New York City native, Born in Harlem New York.

I am a a multidisciplinary Artist and Graphic Designer. My body of work is diverse as are my inspirations.

Initially I used to focus more on comic book style art. I loved the work of Michael Turner and Jim Lee, but in recent years I have fallen more in love with the freedom of illustration, and the refined thought out structures within graphic design.

My personal work is heavily inspired by women of color. Growing up, I didn’t see that many women of color in cartoons and comics. Being able to contribute works to the world that capture a piece of their energy, style, and overall presence is an honor and a privilege.

I am a problem solver at heart. I have always loved math (yes… math), puzzles and riddles. Graphic Design to me is like a creative’s Rubik’s Cube. Being able to take a concept or idea and twist and turn it all sorts of ways, analyzing and learning as you go along. In the end you end up with a completed puzzle with this multiple sides to it that all scream “you’ve succeeded!”

If you’d like to work together, chat about ideas, or simply know more about my work and processes, don’t hesitate to shoot me a message. :)
